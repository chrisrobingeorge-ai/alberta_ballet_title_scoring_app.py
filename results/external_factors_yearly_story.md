# External Factors: Yearly Story

**Dataset:** 10 years with ticket sales data

---

## Correlation Summary

How external factors correlate with ticket sales:

- **Consumer Confidence Prairies**: negative weak correlation (r=-0.000, n=35)
- **Energy Index**: positive weak correlation (r=0.272, n=35)
- **Inflation Adjustment Factor**: negative weak correlation (r=-0.056, n=35)
- **City Median Household Income**: insufficient data for correlation
- **Aud  Engagement Factor**: positive moderate correlation (r=0.318, n=35)
